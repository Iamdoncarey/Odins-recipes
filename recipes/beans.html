Beans recipe

Cooked beans are tender and creamy with a smooth, velvety texture. Their earthy, slightly sweet flavor deepens when seasoned, making them a hearty addition to soups, stews, and salads. They absorb surrounding flavors while maintaining their shape.

Cooked beans are soft and tender, with a creamy interior and slightly firm skin. Their earthy, nutty flavor becomes richer with seasoning, offering a satisfying bite. Perfectly versatile, they complement a variety of dishes, from soups to salads to dips.

Ingredients

  • Dried beans
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Red oil
  • seasonings

Steps

  1. Sort and rinse the beans
  2. Soak the Beans
  3. Boil the beans
  4. Simmer the beans
  5. Test for tenderness
  6. Season the beans
  7. Drain and Serve
  8. Serve
