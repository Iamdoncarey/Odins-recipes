Rice dish

This simple rice recipe creates perfectly fluffy grains by simmering rice in water with a pinch of salt and optional butter. Itâ€™s a versatile, easy side dish that pairs well with nearly any meal, from curries to stir-fries.

With just a few basic ingredients and minimal preparation, this rice recipe is both convenient and delicious. The key to its fluffy texture lies in properly rinsing the rice to remove excess starch and allowing it to steam gently after cooking.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Butter oil or Red Oil

Steps

  1. Rinse the Rice
  2. Boil Water
  3. Add the Rice
  4. Simmer and Cook
